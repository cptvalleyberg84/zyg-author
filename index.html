Brief Bio

The Writing Journey

Rafal Zygula is a visionary writer with a Master’s in Managerial Economics and a background in Public Relations and Sustainability. His debut novel, 'The Age of New Era,' explores humanity's advancement and the future of technology. When not writing, Rafal enjoys painting, experimenting with new technologies, and cherishing time with his family. Join Rafal on a literary journey where imagination meets the wonders of human achievement.

Exploring Depths of Reality

The Everyday Struggle

I write about the everyday struggles of life, a theme prominently featured in my poems. Currently, I’m working on an audio poetry booklet that captures these reflections. However, 'The Age of New Era' is different. This novel idea has been my eternal drive, inspiring me over the past 20 years to learn and hone my writing skills. Along the way, I discovered a passion for poetry, where I channel my thoughts and reflections. Welcome to my world of storytelling, where every piece is a journey through the depths of reality and human experience.
